What is membership system and campaign marketing system

What different of membership and CRM ?

I will describe the part of CRM stands for “Customer Relationship Management” , is a software system that helps business owners easily track all communications and nurture relationships with their leads and clients. Is a system that helps in collecting, organizing, and managing the customer information.

A loyalty program is a system of structured rewards given to customers, usually in exchange for desired behaviors, with the goals of increasing customer loyalty and collecting customer data.

Membership system is new system that has naver been have before , it has adopted the principles of CRM and loyalty program because it would like to nurture relationships with customers. For customers to use again and again.

Membership system uses only two types of loyalty programs : collect points and member-tier.

What is campaign marketing system ?

And not only managing customer relationships campaign marketing sytem is increases the opportunity for customers to use the service. It stimulates and increases sales for the business. Make business successful reach the goal as well but phase division, this makes design of campaign marketing system necessary to go to phase 2.

PROCESS

Requirement Gathering

Step 1 , when I was assigned to create a project I started by requirement gathering from the marketing excutives, by requirement gathering as many needs as possible. which It will be a meeting with the marketing excutives through Google Meet.

Research

Step 2 , I did research study on CRM such as ChocoCRM , Zoho CRM and part about collect points , member-tier. I did research study as well, such as Shopee Reward , The One , Ais previleage , Dtac reward or Kbank etc.

Requirement Analysis

Step 3 , It is the process of analyzing the requirement that I have gathered from the users by analyzing which features are needed, which features are not needed and bring the research data to analyze to meet the needs of system users by providing a requirement specification document and I also analyzed the system design as well.Which this analysis it is very important to start planning for creating and developing. By providing a use case specification document.

Wireframe

Step 4 , before starting the Wireframe, I created an information architecture to organize a group of functions and data. Demonstrates the structure of the system. how the system is structured by creating a site Map format.Then I created a wireframe which I divided into 2 phases. In the first phase I created a wireframe of membership system and campaign marketing system will be in phase 2.
